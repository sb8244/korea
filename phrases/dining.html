Please give me a menu.

Hangeul: 메뉴 좀 갖다주세요.
Romanized: Maenu chom kattajuseyo.

I'd like one order of ...

Hangeul: ... 일 인분 부탁 합니다.
Romanized: ... il-inbun put'k hamnida.

Don't make it hot (spicey).

Hangeul: 맵지 않게 해주세요.
Romanized: Maepchi ahngae haechusaeyo.

Please bring me some water.

Hangeul: 물 좀 주세요.
Romanized: Mul chom chuseyo.

Please bring me the check.

Hangeul: 여기 계산서 좀 가져오세요.
Romanized: Yogi kyesanso chom kacho-oseyo.

Please give me a receipt.

Hangeul: 영수증을 주세요.
Romanized: Yeoungsujeungeul chusaeyo.
